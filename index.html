Benefits of switching

Why homeowners are choosing heat pumps

Beyond the grant, there are lasting reasons to make the switch — for your home, your bills, and the long term.

Significant Energy Savings

Heat pumps typically operate at 300–400% efficiency, producing three to four times more energy than they consume — dramatically reducing your heating bills.

Works in Most UK Homes

Modern heat pumps work reliably in the UK climate and across a wide range of property types — from newer builds to older homes with the right setup.

Low Maintenance

Heat pumps are designed for reliability with minimal servicing requirements — and no annual gas safety checks to arrange once you've made the switch.

Heating and Hot Water

A single system handles both space heating and domestic hot water — replacing your boiler and cylinder in one efficient unit.

Lower Carbon Emissions

Switching from oil or gas to a heat pump significantly reduces your home's carbon footprint — and becomes cleaner as the electricity grid continues to decarbonise.

Long-Term Asset Value

With fossil fuel boilers being phased out, heat pumps are the forward-compatible choice — potentially adding value to your property as the market shifts.
