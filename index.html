Why homeowners are switching

There are lasting reasons to make the move — for your home, your bills and the long term.

Significant Energy Savings

Heat pumps typically run at 300–400% efficiency — producing three to four times more energy than they consume, cutting your heating bills.

Works in Most UK Homes

Modern heat pumps work reliably in the UK climate and across a wide range of property types — from newer builds to older homes with the right setup.

Low Maintenance

Designed for reliability with minimal servicing — and no annual gas safety checks to arrange once you've made the switch.

Heating & Hot Water

A single system handles both space heating and domestic hot water — replacing your boiler and cylinder in one efficient unit.

Lower Carbon Emissions

Switching from oil or gas significantly reduces your home's carbon footprint — and gets cleaner as the grid continues to decarbonise.

Long-Term Asset Value

With fossil fuel boilers being phased out, heat pumps are the forward-compatible choice — potentially adding value as the market shifts.

The Boiler Upgrade Scheme (BUS) is a UK government programme providing grants to homeowners who replace fossil fuel heating with low-carbon alternatives such as heat pumps. The grant is applied directly by the installer — reducing your upfront cost. You don't receive the money yourself; it comes off the total installation price.
Yes. Every installation is carried out by MCS-certified installers — which is a requirement of the Boiler Upgrade Scheme. MCS certification is what allows your grant to be applied, and it's your assurance that the work meets recognised industry standards.
We currently serve homeowners across London and Birmingham with local install teams in both areas. Enter your postcode in the eligibility form and we'll confirm coverage for your specific address right away.
Eligibility depends on a few factors: you need to own the property, have a valid Energy Performance Certificate, and be replacing a fossil fuel heating system. When you enquire, we work through your eligibility in plain English — with no obligation to proceed.
The level of support depends on what you're replacing. The higher amount — up to £9,000 — is specifically for homes currently heated by oil or LPG. Homes replacing gas or other systems may qualify for a different level. We confirm exactly what applies to you during your initial consultation.
